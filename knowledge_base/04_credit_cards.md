# Credit Cards

## Card Products

### Standard Card
- No annual fee
- 16.99% - 24.99% variable APR
- 1% cash back on all purchases
- $200 sign-up bonus after spending $500 in first 3 months

### Rewards Card
- $95 annual fee
- 14.99% - 22.99% variable APR
- 3% cash back on dining and travel, 2% on groceries, 1% on everything else
- $500 sign-up bonus after spending $3,000 in first 3 months
- Includes travel insurance and purchase protection

### Secured Card
- No annual fee
- 22.99% variable APR
- Requires security deposit of $200 - $2,500 (becomes the credit limit)
- Designed for customers building or rebuilding credit
- Automatic review for upgrade to unsecured card after 12 months of on-time payments

## Credit Card Applications

Applications require a credit check (hard inquiry). Approval factors include credit score, income, existing debt, and banking relationship. Instant approval decisions for most applicants. Cards are mailed within 7-10 business days; expedited shipping available for $25.

## Balance Transfers

Balance transfers from other issuers are available at 0% APR for 15 months (3% transfer fee, minimum $5). After the promotional period, the standard APR applies. Balance transfers must be initiated within 60 days of account opening to qualify for the promotional rate.

## Credit Limit Increases

Customers can request a credit limit increase after 6 months of account history. Automatic increases are reviewed annually. A credit limit increase request may result in a hard inquiry. Customers with income changes should update their income information before requesting an increase.

## Lost or Stolen Cards

Report lost or stolen cards immediately by calling the 24/7 hotline at 1-800-555-0199. The card will be blocked instantly. A replacement card is issued free of charge and shipped within 3-5 business days (expedited 1-2 days for $25). Customers are not liable for unauthorized charges reported within 60 days.

## Minimum Payments

Minimum payment is the greater of: $25, or 1% of the balance plus interest and fees. Payments are due 25 days after the statement closing date. Late payment fee is $35 for the first occurrence and $40 for subsequent late payments within 6 billing cycles.
