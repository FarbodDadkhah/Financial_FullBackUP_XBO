# Branch Information and Services

- Hours: Mon-Fri 10:00 AM - 7:00 PM, Sat 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM

- Address: 850 College Avenue
- Hours: Mon-Fri 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

## Appointments

Customers can schedule appointments online at our website or by calling 1-800-555-0100. Walk-ins are welcome but appointments are recommended for:
- Account opening (30 minutes)
- Loan applications (45 minutes)
- Financial planning consultations (60 minutes)
- Safe deposit box access (15 minutes)

## Safe Deposit Boxes

Available at Downtown Main Branch only. Annual rental fees:
- Small (3x5): $50/year
- Medium (5x10): $100/year
- Large (10x10): $200/year

Two keys are provided at rental. Replacement keys cost $25 each. Box drilling (for lost keys) costs $150 plus lock replacement.

## Notary Services

Free notary services for account holders at all branches except University Branch. Non-account holders: $10 per notarization. Notary is available during business hours; no appointment needed but availability depends on notary staff presence.

## Accessibility

All branches are ADA compliant with wheelchair access, accessible ATMs, and hearing loop systems. Translation services available in Spanish, Mandarin, and Vietnamese via phone interpretation. Large-print and Braille documents available upon request with 3-5 business day lead time.
