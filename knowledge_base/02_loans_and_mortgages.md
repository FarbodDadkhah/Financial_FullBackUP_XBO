# Loans and Mortgages

## Personal Loans

Personal loans are available from $1,000 to $50,000 with terms of 12 to 60 months. Interest rates range from 6.99% to 18.99% APR based on creditworthiness. Requirements:
- Minimum credit score of 640
- Proof of income (pay stubs, tax returns, or bank statements)
- Debt-to-income ratio below 43%
- Must be an existing account holder for at least 90 days

Applications are processed within 2-3 business days. Funds are disbursed via direct deposit within 1 business day of approval.

## Auto Loans

Auto loan rates start at 4.49% APR for new vehicles and 5.49% APR for used vehicles. Maximum term is 72 months for new and 60 months for used vehicles. The bank finances up to 100% of the purchase price for new vehicles and up to 90% of the Kelley Blue Book value for used vehicles.

Pre-approval is valid for 60 days. Customers should bring the pre-approval letter to the dealership.

## Home Mortgages

Available mortgage products:
- **30-Year Fixed**: Rates from 6.25% APR. Best for long-term homeowners.
- **15-Year Fixed**: Rates from 5.75% APR. Higher monthly payments but significant interest savings.
- **5/1 ARM**: Starting rate from 5.50% APR, adjusts annually after 5 years. Rate cap of 2% per adjustment, 6% lifetime cap.

Down payment requirements:
- Conventional loans: minimum 5% down (PMI required if under 20%)
- FHA loans: minimum 3.5% down
- VA loans: 0% down for eligible veterans

## Home Equity Lines of Credit (HELOC)

HELOCs allow borrowing against home equity up to 85% of the home's appraised value minus the mortgage balance. Variable rate currently at Prime + 1.5%. Draw period is 10 years followed by a 20-year repayment period. Minimum draw of $500.

## Loan Payment Policies

All loan payments are due on the same day each month as the closing date. A 15-day grace period applies before late fees are charged. Late fee is $35 or 5% of the payment amount, whichever is less. Automatic payments receive a 0.25% rate discount.

Borrowers experiencing hardship may request forbearance for up to 6 months. Contact the loan servicing department at ext. 4500.
